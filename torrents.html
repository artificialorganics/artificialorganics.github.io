Exploring the awe of large amounts of moving liquid.

In VR

There isn't much out there by way of VR liquid simulations, especially in large quantities. The computing power required to generate a simulation in realtime VR simply isn't there yet beyond the research level. For now, stereoscopic 360 renders out of Blender will have to do.

ProTip: Render in winter to save on heating bills!



Blood Flow

Inspired by the scene in The Shining, this short clip is attempt #1 at creating a VR experience with liquid simulation. At 650 frames in stereoscopic 4K (each frame must render twice), this took the better part of a week to render on a single workstation. The clip is in .mp4 format, and plays best on Deo VR or a similar app for your device.
